# Enterprise AI Governance & Ethics Strategy
**AI Advisory Framework for Executive Decision Making**

## 1. Governance Objectives
- **Risk Mitigation:** Identifying bias and toxicity in LLM outputs.
- **Compliance:** Ensuring alignment with EU AI Act and regional regulations.
- **Transparency:** Implementing "LLM-as-a-Judge" for auditability.

## 2. Strategic Implementation
- **Red Teaming:** Continuous adversarial testing of enterprise agents.
- **Model Versioning:** Strict lineage tracking using MLflow.
- **Human-in-the-loop (HITL):** Designating critical nodes where human approval is mandatory.
